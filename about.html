Hi, I'm Marlene Guzman, a software developer with a wildly diverse past and an unshakeable curiosity.

I've never taken the "traditional path" (and honestly, I think that's my superpower). I consider myself a multidisciplinary creator with a background as diverse as my interests. I practice the art of alchemy by blending technolog with creativity. I wholeheartedly believe that this kind of life experience is something I cannot learn in a classroom.

Before writing code, I worked in sales, marketing, and even served as a removal specialist at a funeral home, one of the most meaningful and grounding jobs I've ever had. It was a job that required compassion, remaining calm under pressure, and a deep respect for people and their stories. This experience became a defining part of who I am and shaped how I see people, how I problem-solve, and how I show up in the world. Also, fun fact: long before I entered tech, I unexpectedly stepped in front of the camera: I acted in Spanish-language commercials and was even face-scanned for the Spiderman video game on PS4. Yes, my digital alter ego once stood next to Peter Parker.

Now, I've found my place in software development, where all the pieces of my background come together: the storyteller from acting, the strategy from marketing, the empathy from funeral work, and the logic from engineering. I'm building a life that blends creativity, clarity, and code, and I'm excited for whatever comes next.

A Few Things I Believe

  • Growth isn't linear, but passion always pays forward.
  • Curiosity is a career strategy.
  • Your weird, winding path is your advantage.
