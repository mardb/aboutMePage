Featured Project

RISE (Resources for Immigration Support & Emergencies)

A passion project I built to help immigrants quickly access the resources, rights, and emergency guidance they may need, all in one place, and without the overwhelm and confusion. It started as a simple question: what if the critical information people desperately need in stressful moments wasn't buried under ten tabs and legal jargon and spread across different websites?

I designed RISE to be clean, multilingual, easy-to-navigate, centralized tool. The "Know Your Rights" card is built to be understood at a glance and usable in real situations. RISE is part practical guide, part safety net, and one of the things I'm most proud of building. (WIP)
